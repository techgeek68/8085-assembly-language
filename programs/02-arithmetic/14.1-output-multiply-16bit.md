---
* **Multiplicand (16-bit)** is stored at:
  * `4000H` > Low byte
  * `4001H` > High byte
    
* **Multiplier (16-bit)** is stored at:
  * `4002H` > Low byte
  * `4003H` > High byte
    
* **Product (32-bit)** is stored at:
  * `2000H` > High word (Low byte)
  * `2001H` > High word (High byte)
  * `2002H` > Low word (Low byte)
  * `2003H` > Low word (High byte)
---

- Input 1: `0005H × 0006H=0000001EH`

| Address | Value | Description       |
| ------- | ----- | ----------------- |
| 4000H   | 05H   | Multiplicand Low  |
| 4001H   | 00H   | Multiplicand High |
| 4002H   | 06H   | Multiplier Low    |
| 4003H   | 00H   | Multiplier High   |


- Output: 
| Address | Value | Description         |
| ------- | ----- | ------------------- |
| 2000H   | 00H   | High Word Low Byte  |
| 2001H   | 00H   | High Word High Byte |
| 2002H   | 1EH   | Low Word Low Byte   |
| 2003H   | 00H   | Low Word High Byte  |
